Welcome to Auer Concierge — personalized recovery care designed to support your healing, comfort, and confidence. Led by Heather Auer, a licensed Occupational Therapist, Certified Lymphedema Therapist (CLT), and Certified Cosmetic Surgery Therapist (CCST), Auer Concierge blends clinical expertise with compassionate, concierge-level care for every stage of recovery.

Our Founder

The Care Behind Auer Concierge

Heather Auer brings advanced training and clinical expertise in Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD), Complete Decongestive Therapy (CDT), scar management, therapeutic exercise, compression garment assessment, and cosmetic surgery recovery.

Through Auer Concierge, Heather provides personalized care plans tailored to each client’s individual goals, needs, and stage of healing. She works closely with clients recovering from cosmetic, reconstructive, and medical procedures to help reduce swelling, improve mobility, support healing, and promote optimal recovery.

Heather also offers specialized support for individuals managing lymphedema, as well as those navigating cancer treatment, survivorship, and recovery.
